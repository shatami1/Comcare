Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver the same day?

Yes! For orders placed before 12 PM, we offer same-day delivery in the Atlanta area. Call us directly for availability.

What if I need to return equipment early?

No problem! Cancellations more than 24 hours in advance receive a full refund. For last-minute cancellations, we may charge one day's rental.

Do you provide cleaning services?

All equipment is professionally cleaned and sanitized before delivery. After your rental, standard cleaning is included in the rental fee.

What if equipment breaks during my rental?

Contact us immediately. We'll either repair or replace the equipment at no additional cost to you.

What is the minimum rental period?

Minimum rental is 1 day. We offer daily, weekly, and monthly rates with discounts for longer rentals.

Is a deposit required?

A credit card is required to secure your booking. Deposits vary by equipment type (typically 10-20% of rental cost).
